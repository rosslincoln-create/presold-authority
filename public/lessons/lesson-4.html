Your Authority Positioning

5–7 minutes · Builder: Authority Positioning Generator
▶

Lesson video coming soon

Your positioning statement is the foundation everything else builds on. It is what prospects read before they call you, what your LinkedIn headline reflects, and what your content reinforces. When it is right, serious buyers and sellers arrive already leaning toward you. When it is vague, you get compared on commission.

Most agents never define this clearly. They describe what they do — "I help buyers and sellers in the metro area" — rather than who they serve, what outcome they deliver, and why their process is the safe choice. The result is forgettable copy that blends into every other profile on the page.

In this lesson you will generate your positioning statement using your Context Card as the source of truth. The builder produces five options — you choose the one that feels most accurate, then keep the differentiators and boundary statement that go with it.

What this builder produces

  • 1 primary positioning statement — your "I help X achieve Y by Z" sentence, ready to deploy
  • 5 alternatives — different angles on the same positioning so you can pick the best fit
  • 3 safe-choice differentiators — process-based reasons why serious clients choose you
  • 10 proof cues — credibility signals you can truthfully use across your profile and content
  • 1 boundary statement — a professional line that repels tire-kickers without sounding harsh
Choose the statement that is most accurate, not the one that sounds most impressive. Believable beats brilliant. Your differentiators should describe your process, not your personality.

Once you have saved your positioning, it feeds directly into Lesson 5 where the Profile Install builder uses it to rewrite your LinkedIn headline and About section.
