The Context Card

3–5 minutes · Builder: Context Card Review
▶

Lesson video coming soon

Everything this platform generates — posts, DMs, scripts, assets — pulls from one place: your Context Card. It is the single source of truth for who you are, who you serve, how you work, and how you sound. When the Context Card is dialed in, outputs stay consistent. When it is empty or vague, the system has nothing specific to anchor to.

Without that context, AI defaults to generic real-estate language: helpful, but not yours. Prospects get copy that could belong to any agent in your market — which is exactly the interchangeability problem the first two lessons named. The Context Card fixes that at the root: one structured profile, reused everywhere.

You fill it once. After that, every prompt, every builder, and every generated asset reads from the same facts, positioning, proof, compliance details, and voice. You are not re-explaining yourself on every task; you are executing against a profile the platform already understands.

What the Context Card covers

The card is organised into sections so nothing important falls through the cracks:

  • Basics — Your name, market, and role so outputs are grounded in reality.
  • Ideal client — Who you actually want to work with so messaging stays relevant.
  • Your positioning — How you explain your process and edge in a few clear steps.
  • Proof & credibility — Results and signals that justify trust before the first call.
  • Contact & compliance — Brokerage and contact details so disclosures stay correct.
  • Voice & tone — How you want to sound so the writing matches you, not a template.
A good enough Context Card beats a perfect one that never gets finished. Fill it in, deploy it, refine it later.
