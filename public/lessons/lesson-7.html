From Authority to Inbound Conversations

Publishing authority content is how prospects find you and begin to trust you before they make contact. But content without a conversion path leaves the process unfinished. In this lesson, you build both sides: the posts that attract serious attention, and the DM flow that turns that attention into calm, qualified conversations.

Part A generates 10 authority posts built around your content pillars — each one structured to attract decision-stage buyers and sellers and subtly filter out tire-kickers. You will also receive 10 alternative hooks you can use to test different angles on the same topics.

Part B generates your inbound DM conversation path — the sequence you use when someone responds to your content or reaches out. This is not a sales script. It is a calm, structured path from first contact to a qualified conversation, written in your tone and for your market.
