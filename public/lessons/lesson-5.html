What Your Profile Is Actually For

Most agents treat their LinkedIn profile like a résumé — a record of where they've worked and what they've done. That framing produces profiles that feel passive and interchangeable. A profile built for authority works differently: it's a trust asset. Every section — your headline, your About copy, your pinned post — is working to answer the question a serious prospect is silently asking: 'Is this the right person for me?'

In this lesson, you'll generate three connected outputs: a headline that signals exactly who you serve, an About section that reads like a calm expert introducing themselves, and a pinned post that gives prospects one place to understand you before they ever reach out. These aren't decoration. They're the first layer of the pre-sold experience — the part that happens before the conversation starts.

Your positioning statement from Lesson 4 is the foundation. The AI will use it alongside your context card to write profile copy that's consistent, specific to your market, and ready to deploy.
